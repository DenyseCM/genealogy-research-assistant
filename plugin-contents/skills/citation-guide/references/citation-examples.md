# Citation Examples by Record Type

Complete citation examples showing the Jones five-question framework applied to common genealogical sources.

## Census Records

**Full reference note:**
1870 U.S. census, Lancaster County, Pennsylvania, population schedule, Earl Township, p. 12 (stamped), dwelling 87, family 91, line 22, Peter Erb household; digital image, Ancestry.com (https://www.ancestry.com : accessed 11 March 2026); citing NARA microfilm publication M593, roll 1341.

**Breakdown:**
- Who: United States Bureau of the Census
- What: 1870 U.S. census, population schedule
- When: 1870 (enumeration)
- Where is: Digital image at Ancestry.com, citing NARA microfilm
- Where in: Earl Township, p. 12, dwelling 87, family 91, line 22
